About

Meet the maker

😊

Hi! I’m Lois, an 11‑year‑old crocheter who loves bright colours, soft yarns, and learning new stitches. I started with granny squares and now I’m trying amigurumi and simple wearables. When I’m not crocheting, I’m reading, drawing, or baking cookies.

On this site you’ll find a gallery of my projects, a journal with behind‑the‑scenes notes, and links to patterns or places to buy (maybe in the future!).
